The Deprogrammer

A playlist editor and gameplay companion app for the Deprogrammed podcast. This was my solo project during EDA that included a week of planning and 2 weeks of development. Users can edit a list of songs for an artist down to a top 20 and "play" the Deprogrammed game: where a panel of 3-4 judges determines an artist's top 10 songs.

the movies saga

An app for logging and storing a movie collection. Users can add and edit movies, as well as click on the posters on the main gallery page to view details for that movie. This was a weekend project to show off everything we learned during the week we first covered Redux Sagas. I'm really satisified with the Material-UI styling on this one.

Gallery of my Life

A simple Instagram-style CRUD app where users can click on posted images to see captions, as well as posting images and captions, adding "likes" to images, and deleting items. This was our weekend project during React week.
